mosAIc: Next-Generation Performance Assessment Builder

mosAIc is a powerful, AI-assisted platform designed for Kentucky teachers, helping them quickly, easily, and effectively create performance-based assessments that align with vibrant learning expectations.

Inspired by the idea of a mosaic—where many individual pieces come together to create a complete and meaningful whole—mosAIc brings together performance artifacts and assessment elements to build a full picture of a student's learning profile, focused on real-world readiness and skill mastery.

At its core, mosAIc is a streamlined, teacher-centered chatbot tool. Teachers simply enter specific information into guided fields, and AI generates a customized, performance-based assessment. Importantly, mosAIc ensures that teacher expertise and judgment remain central — educators are encouraged to review, refine, and personalize the AI’s suggestions to ensure they are appropriate and aligned to student needs.

The goal of mosAIc is simple:
Make designing vibrant, real-world, performance-based assessments easier, faster, more effective, and more innovative — while keeping teachers in control.
